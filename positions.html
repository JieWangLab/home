For Researchers

Wang Lab is an interdisciplinary team with diverse experiences in biology, statistics, computer science, etc. We are seeking highly motivated postdoctoral fellows, assistant researchers, and associated researchers.


  • Postdoctoral Fellows

    • Responsibilities: Participate in conceiving projects, conduct projects, and apply for scientific fundings.

    Qualifications: Candidates are expected to have a Ph.D. degree in bioinformatics, statistics, computer science, cell biology, regenerative medicine, or relevant fields. We seek candidates who graduated within the past two years and had academic publications in high-profile journals. (2) Candidates with experience in single-cell sequencing analysis, software development, deep learning, CRISPR technology, or organoid technology are preferred. (3) High passion and motivation for science, positive attitudes to work and to learn new knowledge, high responsibility and team spirit, good communication and writing skills in English or Chinese.

    Salary and benefits: (1) > 280,000 RMB / year. (2) Postdoctoral positions are special research assistants in the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Outstanding postdoctoral fellows can apply for associated researchers/professors. (3) Provide enough research funding and a top-tier platform. Provide subsidy and reward according to work and contribution. (4) We support postdoctoral fellows to apply for national and local talent programs which have additional benefits. For example, Guangzhou high-class talent will be rewarded 1-3 million RMB. (5) Pay a high-standard insurance, and have an annual holiday with salary. Help to solve issues in life including the residence, household, school of children and so on.


  • Assistant Researchers

    • Responsibilities: Conduct projects according to the specificity of candidates.

    Qualifications: (1) Master or higher degree in bioinformatics, biology, regenerative medicine, mathematics, etc. (2) We prefer candidates who have experience in computer programming, biological omics data analysis, animal experiments or organoid culture. (3) High responsibility and team spirit, good communication and writing skills in English or Chinese. (4) Encourage candidates to apply for Ph.D. candidates or other promotions.

    Salary and benefits: (1) A competitive salary will be provided. (2) Refer to the benefits for postdoctoral fellows.


  • Associated Researchers

    • Responsibilities: Propose and conduct projects, and apply for scientific fundings and talent programs.

    Qualifications: (1) Ph.D. in bioinformatics, regenerative biology, etc. is required. We prefer candidates who have strong publications in high-profile journals. (2) We seek candidates who had > 2 years postdoctoral experience, and < 40 years old. Outstanding candidates do not need to follow the restrictions. (3) We prefer candidates who can independently conduct the research. (4) High responsibility and team spirit, good communication and writing skills in English or Chinese.

    Salary and benefits: (1) A competitive salary will be discussed in the interview. (2) Refer to the benefits for postdoctoral fellows. (3) The position is officially authorized by the Chinese Academy of Sciences.


    Apply: Please send your CV, reference letters, research summary and other relevant materials to my email with the title “position + your name” (wang_jie01{at}gibh.ac.cn).
