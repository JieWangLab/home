For Researchers

Wang lab focuses on disclosing the principles of tissue regeneration through biological omics measurement, bioinformatics analysis, and functional experiments. We are an interdisciplinary team with strong experience in biology, statistics, bioinformatics, etc. We seek highly motivated postdoctoral fellows, associated researchers and assistant researchers.


  • Postdoctoral Fellows

    • Responsibilities: Participate in conceiving projects, conduct projects, and apply scientific fundings.

    Qualifications: Candidates are expected to have a Ph.D. degree in bioinformatics, statistics, cell biology, regenerative medicine or relevant majors. We seek candidates who graduated within the past two years and had publications in high-profile journals. (2) Candidates with experience in single-cell sequencing analysis, software development, CRISPR technology, or organoid technology are preferred. (3) High passion and high motivation for science, positive attitudes to work and to learn new knowledge, high responsibility and team spirit, good communication and writing skills in Chinese and English.

    Salary and benefits: (1) > 300,000 RMB / year. (2) Postdoctoral positions are managed as the special research assistants in the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Outstanding postdoctoral fellows can apply for associated researchers/professors. (3) Provide enough research funding and a top-tier platform. Provide subsidy and reward according to work and contribution. (4) We support applying for national and local talent programs which have additional benefits. For example, Guangzhou high-class talent will be rewarded 1-3 million RMB. (5) Pay a high-standard insurance, and have annual holiday with salary. Assist to solve issues in life including the residence, household, school of children and so on.


  • Associated Researchers

    • Responsibilities: Propose and conduct projects, and apply scientific fundings and talent programs.

    Qualifications: (1) Ph.D. in bioinformatics, regenerative biology, etc. is required. We prefer candidates who have publications in high-profile journals. (2) We seek candidates who had > 2 years postdoctoral experience, and < 40 years old. Outstanding candidates do not need to follow these restrictions. (3) We prefer candidates who are able to independently conduct the research. (4) High responsibility and team spirit, good communication and writing skills in Chinese and English.

    Salary and benefits: (1) A competitive salary will be discussed in the interview. (2) The position is officially authorized by the Chinese Academy of Sciences. (3) Refer to the benefits for postdoctoral fellows.
